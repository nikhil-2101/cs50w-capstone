Overview

Welcome to the help page of EasyTicket. Here you can find useful information and FAQs to help you navigate through our platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?

To create an event, go to the dashboard and click on 'Create Event'. Fill in the required details such as event title, description, date, and location.

Can I edit my event after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your event details any time before the event starts. Go to the event detail page and click on 'Edit Event'.

How do I book tickets for an event?

On the event detail page, select the date and click 'Book a Seat'. Fill in the number of seats and proceed to payment.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa and MasterCard.

How do I save my eticket?

Click on Ctrl-P/Cmd-P when you are in your eticket page to save it to your device by changing your destination to Save as PDF! Alternatively, you can screenshot your page. Do ensure that the QR code can be seen!
